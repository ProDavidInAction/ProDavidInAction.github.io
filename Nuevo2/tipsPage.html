Here Are Some Tips!

1) Study Smartly!

You may think you know how to study. After all, you made it all the way through Y9 probably using the same study method. However, as the level increases, your study method may not be as good as it seems any more. It may be time to reconsider, as studying the night before the exam wont give you a pass. Generally, there are 4 types of learners, visual learners, auditory learners, kinesthetic learners and reading and writing learners. Test yourself in each of these, as finding how you learn best may be the road for your academic succes and future achievements in life.

2) Be emotionally intelligent!

Nowadays, most modern entrepeneurs and CEO's build their road to success on emotional inteligence. You may have memorized the book from a to z and can recite every page, phrase and letter correctly, but, when it comes to the exam, if you get stressed out, it may be a cause for you to forget most of what you have worked hard to gain. This might lead to whoevers next to you beating in terms of score although he may not have studied as much. The same applies later in life, when you are trying to build up your portfolio to gain a reputation, if you are not emotionally intelligent, it won't happen;

3) Be respectfull!

Respect is one of the best things that you can havve towards your teachers, and, it will indirectly move you in the correct direction for them to get a linking on you. Studies have shown that students who behave best are usually the students with the highest scores in the exams, due to the teacher paying more attention to their exam as they want to help you get a pass. In addition, if you show respect, teachers will respect you back, and, if you are in a situation where a sanction can be given to you, you may just as well avoid it.
